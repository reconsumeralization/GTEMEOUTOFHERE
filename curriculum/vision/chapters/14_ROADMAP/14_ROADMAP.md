### Field Note (The Analyst)
Roadmaps that ignored security-first phases became headlines. The winning pattern: prototype with MFA/least privilege/sandboxed helpers; pilot with family cohorts to tune safety; scale tribes and ethical marketplaces; then push MAS standards. The future state is CSA-like: defend everyone, build transparency, and keep AI advisory, not authoritative.

### Threat Model
- Scaling without guardrails: security debt carried into pilots/partners.
- Downstream/vendor risk: unverified integrations; unremediated vulns.
- Drift from advisor to authority as features expand.

### Mitigation Checklist
- Phase-gate with security: MFA/least privilege, sandboxed helpers, SBOM/signatures, pinned deps/actions.
- Pilot with small cohorts; iterate on safety lenses and grounding protocols.
- Enforce advisor-not-authority at each phase; monitor drift; publish MAS-aligned standards and transparency practices.
