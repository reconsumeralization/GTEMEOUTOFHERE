### Trust & Delivery Principles
- Scientific reliability: documented methods, solid data, interdisciplinary review; conclusions traceable and sound.

- Human-centered innovation: clear interfaces, understandable outputs, meaningful support for underwriting/risk/claims/customer comms.
- Responsibility & integrity: reduce real-world harm, clarify risk, strengthen resilience; handle data and impacts responsibly.
